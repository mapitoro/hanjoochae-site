## Reference style (the format to follow)

Each entry follows this style (matches `publications.json` fields):

- **authors** — full first-name last-name order, comma-separated, `and` before the
  last author. Write Han Joo Chae's name in full as printed on the paper.
  e.g. `Han Joo Chae, Ji-Been Kim, Gwanmo Park, David M. O'Sullivan, Jinwook Seo, and Jung-Jun Park`
- **year** — four-digit integer.
- **title** — full title, Title Case as published.
- **venue** — full venue name with a parenthetical short name + two-digit year:
  `IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR '22)`.
- **type** — `Conference` or `Journal` (full papers only).
- **link** — DOI URL preferred (`https://doi.org/...`).
- **projectPage** *(optional)* — project/demo page URL.
