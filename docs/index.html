Problem

Radiation therapy planning depends on manual organ and tumor contouring on CT/MR — slow, variable between clinicians, and a bottleneck to treating more patients.

Approach

OncoStudio auto-contours organs-at-risk and tumors in about a minute through a zero-click workflow integrated with PACS, TPS, and EMR. It anchors a full platform — OncoFlow, OncoStudio, OncoPlan, and OncoRPT — spanning imaging, planning, delivery, and follow-up.

Impact

Captured 25% domestic market share and ranked #1 in clinical performance benchmarks by leading Japanese research institutions, with 300,000+ patient cases processed. I lead R&D and global expansion across the US, Latin America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

Flagship Career Impact · 2020–2023

AI Cloud Platform for 3D-Bioprinted Wound Regeneration

ROKIT Healthcare · CAIO & Vice President · 2020–2023

An AI platform that turns a single tablet photo of a wound into a ready-to-print 3D patch model in seconds — then bio-prints it at the point of care to treat diabetic foot ulcers, deployed across the US, Asia, the Middle East, and Latin America.

Problem

Personalized regenerative patches require precise scanning, CAD modeling, and bioprinting — work that a surgical team anywhere in the world cannot realistically do at the point of care. To reach patients globally, the hard part had to disappear into software.

Approach

We built an AI platform (AiD Regen) that, from a single tablet RGB-D photo, segments the wound, reconstructs its 3D surface, and generates a ready-to-print 3D model in about 30 seconds. Production-grade MLOps on Google Cloud (Vertex AI, GKE) delivers identical quality at any clinical site — no on-site AI specialist required.

Impact

Founded and scaled the AI department from zero to 15, achieved both FDA and CE clearance, and drove the full commercial cycle from MVP to $1B+ in long-term contracts across the US, India, Turkey, the Middle East, and Latin America. Launched on AWS in 6 months, then migrated to Google Cloud in 3 — the work became a featured Google Cloud customer story.
